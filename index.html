PayFlow

FinTech • Mobile App 2024 Lead UX Designer

Overview

PayFlow is a mobile banking app designed specifically for Gen Z users who want simple, intuitive financial management without the complexity of traditional banking apps. The challenge was to make personal finance engaging and accessible while maintaining security and compliance standards.

The Challenge

Traditional banking apps have notoriously poor user experiences, with cluttered interfaces and confusing navigation. Our research showed that 68% of Gen Z users felt intimidated by existing banking apps. We needed to create an experience that felt modern, trustworthy, and effortless.

Research & Discovery

I conducted 24 user interviews and 3 design workshops with Gen Z participants. Key findings revealed that users wanted instant transaction notifications, visual spending breakdowns, and easy money transfers. Security was important, but it needed to feel invisible rather than obtrusive.

Solution

The final design featured a card-based interface with gesture controls, real-time spending insights using AI, and a social payment feature. We reduced the account opening process from 15 minutes to 3 minutes through smart form design and progressive disclosure.

Results

After launch, PayFlow achieved a 4.8 star rating with over 500K downloads in the first quarter. User engagement increased by 180%, and the app won "Best FinTech Design" at the 2024 UX Awards.

MediConnect

Healthcare • Web Platform 2023 Senior UX Designer

Overview

MediConnect is a telemedicine platform that connects patients with healthcare providers through secure video consultations, digital prescriptions, and integrated health records. The goal was to make healthcare more accessible while maintaining HIPAA compliance.

The Problem

During the pandemic, healthcare providers struggled with fragmented communication tools. Patients were frustrated with long wait times and complicated booking systems. We needed to create a seamless experience for both sides.

Design Process

I led a team of 3 designers through extensive user research with both patients and doctors. We created detailed user journeys, conducted usability testing with 50+ participants, and iterated through 5 major design versions based on feedback.

Key Features

Smart appointment scheduling with automatic provider matching, secure video consultations with screen sharing, integrated health records with timeline view, and digital prescription delivery to local pharmacies.

Impact

MediConnect now serves 200+ healthcare providers and 50,000+ patients. Patient satisfaction scores increased to 92%, and providers reported saving 3+ hours per day on administrative tasks.
